How to play

Movement

  • w - move up
  • d - move right
  • s - move down
  • a - move left
  • [space bar] - dash

Purchase

How to purchase a weapon.

Once you have enough cash to purchase a weapon, the price will turn form red to green.

Clicking the corresponding key bind will purchase that weapon.

  • 2 -> AK47
  • 3 -> RPG

How to purchase an upgrade

The upgrade price for each upgrade cost varies depending on your current weapon.

The key bind for each upgrade is the letter that becomes visible once you have enough money to purchase the upgrade.

Clicking the corresponding key binds will purchase that upgrade.

  • v -> Increase Ammo
  • b -> Decrease Fire Rate
  • n -> Decrease Reload Time
  • m -> Increase Player Health

Strategy

Survive as long as possible.

Purchasing health (m) will go a long way.
